INTRODUCTION

Since its opening in August 2008, The Deck Saigon has become one of the leading restaurants in Vietnam and attracts a stylish crowd who come for serious food and cocktails in a seriously stylish venue. Its fabulous river side location and unique style make it one of the most popular destinations in Ho Chi Minh City. There is no other location like this in Asia’s most exciting and chic city.
Tell us about your event and let us create a unique experience for you and your guests. From sophisticated events with extra lighting, video projection, DJ or Jazz band, our selection of exquisite canapés, Champagne and oyster bar to more casual barbecue events, The Deck Saigon is the place to be in Ho Chi Minh City!
You can also bring The Deck Saigon to your home! Our contemporary pan-Asian fusion cuisine and delightful cocktails can be served by our professional and friendly team in the comfort of your own house or corporate office. We can also create the perfect garden party in your own backyard with our portable bar and barbecue. We will do the work, just invite your family and friends and enjoy!

PRIVATE & SPECIAL EVENTS

The Deck Saigon offers a flexible open air event space on the banks of the Saigon River suitable for all types of private events. From intimate celebrations to large receptions for up to 300 people and dinners for up to 150 people, our team will create the right atmosphere for you and your guests to enjoy and relax. Our professional and friendly team will be there to help you every step of the way, from initial enquiry right through to the actual event.

CORPORATE EVENTS

The Deck Saigon offers the perfect setting for your corporate events. Enchant your colleagues and clients by hosting your function in our stunning venue, with the most beautiful river as a backdrop. Our flexible facilities can cater for seated or buffet dinners, product launches, press events, fashion shows, film shoots. With unbeatable natural light enhanced by the Saigon River, beautiful garden, private bar and top class food, our professional and friendly team will make your event one to remember!

WEDDINGS

Make your special day even more special by tying the knot in a venue like no other! Our stunning scenery will create the best backdrop for your wedding celebration, and our Event Team will do the rest, going above and beyond to ensure your event runs to perfection and exceeds your expectations. Let our Event Team deal with the tiny details – from flower arrangements, to décor and music – and our Head Chef tailor-design a menu that will delight everyone. Just relax and enjoy your day!!!

GOURMET CATERING

Why not bring The Deck Saigon to your home! Let our Chef create an individualised menu for your corporate or special event and our mixologists bring our famous cocktails to your party. Whether it is a wedding celebration, drinks reception, or barbecue, The Deck Saigon can cater for almost any occasion. We can provide a full catering package, to include food, portable bar and drinks, serving staff, even portable barbecue and decorations or only drop off our beautifully presented food to your door for you to serve and impress your guests!
Please contact our experienced and friendly team to request a full proposal.

ARRIVE BY BOAT

PRIVATE BOAT TAXI
Arrive to The Deck in style by boat direct to the dining room steps. The only restaurant in Saigon where you can arrive by your own private speedboat.
COCKTAIL CRUISE
Capture the life on the banks of the Saigon River for a pre-dinner sunset cruise. Enjoy our chef’s selection of canapés. Ideal for romantic evenings, family outings or dinner with friends.
CU CHI TUNNELS
The Cu Chu Tunnels are an immense network of connecting underground tunnels used during the time of the Vietnam War. The Deck “Cu Chi Experience” will guide you and your guests by private speedboat to the site of The tunnels. You will be accompanied by our knowledgable tour guides all at your own pace. A light breakfast will be served on-board before entering the tunnels, picnic lunch and ice cold rose wine.
DUTCH BOAT
Ideal for corporate team building or a group outing. Made by hand in Holland cruise along the Mekong River while enjoying our selection of canapés and Rose. BBQ also now available!
LUXURY YACHT
One of the only 2 level luxury Yachts for rent in Saigon. For up to 13 people you and your guests can cruise the Mekong while sipping your favourite libations and sampling our chef’s selection of canapés. Bespoke this boat excursion to your hearts desire!

BOAT EXPERIENCE

Arrive in style – moor your boat at our dock – walk directly up the steps into The Deck Saigon.
If you would like to arrive in style we will organise a boat to pick you up from down town. You can also start your party on a boat by booking a one hour Private Sunset Cruise and enjoy the beautiful scenery whilst sipping a glass of rosé wine and savouring a selection of mouthwatering canapés. Alternatively we are only a 15 minute drive from the centre. Please contact us on info@thedecksaigon.vnfor further information
